Our Story

EgyptianGlow was founded in Kenya with one mission: to help women feel confident in their own natural beauty. Our products are carefully curated to provide glowing, radiant skin — because every woman deserves to shine beautifully.

We believe in affordable luxury, high quality, and genuine self-care. Whether you shop online or visit our pickup point in Nairobi’s CBD, we’re always ready to help you glow!
